About CramCrew

Connecting students. Building better study habits.

Our Mission

CramCrew was created to solve a simple but common problem: students struggle to stay motivated when studying alone. Our goal is to make studying more social, engaging, and effective by connecting students with the right study partners.

The Problem

Many students face the same challenges:

• Lack of motivation

• Difficulty finding people in the same classes

• Studying alone for long periods

Our Solution

CramCrew matches students based on:

📚 Subjects & classes

⏰ Availability

🤝 Study style preferences

By keeping the platform simple and free, we make it easy for anyone to quickly find a study partner or group.

Our Vision

We believe studying shouldn't feel isolating. Our vision is to build a community where students support each other, stay accountable, and succeed together.
